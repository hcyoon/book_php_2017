# 3-21. 섭씨를 화씨로 변환

source: `{{ page.path }}`

```php
<?
	echo "-------------------------------<br>";
	echo "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;섭씨&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;화씨<br>";
	echo "-------------------------------<br>";

	for($c=-15; $c<=35; $c=$c+5) {
		$f=$c * 9 / 5 + 32;
		echo "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; $c &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; $f<br>";
	}

	echo "-------------------------------<br>";
?>
```


결과 :
```
-------------------------------
   섭씨   화씨
-------------------------------
    -15     5
    -10     14
    -5     23
    0     32
    5     41
    10     50
    15     59
    20     68
    25     77
    30     86
    35     95
-------------------------------
```
